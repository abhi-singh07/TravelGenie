Here is a travel itinerary for Srinagar, Ladakh, and Shimla:

**Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar, Ladakh**

Welcome to the breathtaking beauty of Ladakh! You arrive at Kushok Bakula Rimpochee Airport (IXZ) in the morning. After checking into your hotel, you have the rest of the day free to explore the city.

Start your journey with a visit to the iconic Shankaracharya Temple, which offers stunning views of the city and the surrounding mountains. Take a leisurely walk around the temple complex and enjoy the serene atmosphere.

In the afternoon, head to the Hazratbal Shrine, a sacred site for Hindus and Muslims alike. Marvel at the beautiful architecture and the tranquil gardens surrounding the shrine.

As the day comes to an end, indulge in a delicious local dinner and get a good night's rest.

**Day 2: Srinagar**

Begin your day with a visit to the famous Dal Lake, also known as Nagin Lake. Take a scenic boat ride on the lake and enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Next, head to the Mughal Gardens, a series of beautiful gardens built by the Mughal emperors in the 16th century. Explore the intricate pathways, reflecting pools, and lush greenery.

After lunch, visit the St. Mary's Church, one of the oldest churches in India. Take a moment to appreciate the historic architecture and the serene atmosphere.

In the evening, enjoy a traditional Ladakhi dinner at a local restaurant and take a stroll around the city, taking in the sights and sounds of this beautiful city.

**Day 3: Srinagar to Leh**

After breakfast, depart for Leh, the capital city of Ladakh (approximately 2.5 hours). Stop at the scenic village of Chisumle on the way, which is famous for its ancient monastery and stunning views.

Upon arrival in Leh, check into your hotel and take some time to rest and acclimate to the high altitude.

In the afternoon, visit the Thiksey Monastery, one of the most impressive monasteries in Ladakh. Marvel at the stunning architecture and the vibrant murals depicting Buddhist scriptures.

**Day 4: Leh**

Begin your day with a visit to the Hemis National Park, home to the majestic Himalayan brown bears (seasonal). Take a guided trek through the park's lush meadows and forests.

Next, head to the Spituk Monastery, which houses a stunning collection of Buddhist artifacts and frescoes. Explore the monastery's intricate architecture and take in the serene atmosphere.

After lunch, visit the Leh Palace, which offers breathtaking views of the city and surrounding mountains. Take a stroll around the palace complex and explore its beautiful courtyards and gardens.

In the evening, enjoy a traditional Ladakhi dinner at a local restaurant and take some time to rest and acclimate to the high altitude.

**Day 5: Leh**

Begin your day with a visit to the Phanang Lhatsun Choekor Monastery, one of the oldest monasteries in Ladakh. Marvel at the stunning architecture and the vibrant murals depicting Buddhist scriptures.

Next, head to the Magnetic Hill, where you can experience the strange phenomenon of vehicles rolling uphill (seasonal). Take a guided tour around the hill and learn about its fascinating history.

After lunch, visit the Leh Market, where you can buy local handicrafts and souvenirs. Explore the vibrant stalls and take in the sights and sounds of this bustling market.

In the evening, enjoy a farewell dinner at a local restaurant and depart for your onward journey to Shimla (approximately 8 hours).

**Day 6: Arrival in Shimla**

Welcome to the charming town of Shimla! You arrive at Shimla Airport (SLA) in the morning. After checking into your hotel, you have the rest of the day free to explore the city.

Start your journey with a visit to the famous Mall Road, which is lined with shops, restaurants, and street performers. Take a leisurely walk along the road and enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding mountains.

In the afternoon, head to the Viceregal Lodge, a historic mansion built in 1892 for the British Viceroy of India. Explore the mansion's beautiful architecture and take in the serene atmosphere.

After lunch, visit the Indian Institute of Advanced Study, which is home to some of the finest art and artifacts from across India. Take a stroll around the institute's beautiful gardens and explore its stunning architecture.

In the evening, enjoy a delicious local dinner at a restaurant near your hotel and take some time to rest and relax in this beautiful city.

**Day 7: Shimla**

Begin your day with a visit to the Kalka - Shimla Railway, which offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains. Take a scenic train ride and marvel at the stunning scenery.

Next, head to the Annandale Ground, where you can enjoy a leisurely walk around the beautiful gardens and take in the serene atmosphere. Visit the nearby St. Michael's Church, one of the oldest churches in India.

After lunch, visit the St. Mary's Church, which is famous for its beautiful stained-glass windows and intricate architecture. Take a moment to appreciate the historic significance of this church.

In the evening, enjoy a farewell dinner at a local restaurant and depart for your onward journey, taking with you the memories of this incredible trip to Srinagar, Ladakh, and Shimla!
